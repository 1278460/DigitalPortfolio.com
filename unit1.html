Unit 1 - Online world


INTRODUCTION

On this unit, pass, merit and distinction is determined from taking a set exam, unlike the other units where it is determined by coursework. This unit is gaining knowledge about the modern online world, online services, the range of the online services including: email, online data storage, collaborative software, search engines and blogging. Also in this unit we have to investigate the technology and software that support the online services.


We had to gather as much information as we can from three learning aims: a, b and c. In learning aim a covers online services, documents and communication. Learning aim b covers the internet, worldwide web, email, data exchange and data storage. And whereas learning aim c covers the possible threats to data. The exam paper doesn’t always cover everything that we learned as there’s a limited number of topics to fit in the 1 hour exam.
